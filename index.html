Introduction

The Advanced Sustainable Technology International Conference (ASTECH) 2024 marks the collaborative initiation between Politeknik Kuching Sarawak (PKS) Malaysia and Politeknik Negeri Banding (POLBAN) Indonesia, and is a significant milestone as the first international conference organized by POLBAN and co-organized by PKS. Set to take place from August 5th to 6th in Kuching Sarawak Malaysia, this distinguished assembly aspires to bring together global academics, scholars, researchers, and industry experts, fostering a dynamic research and innovation ecosystem. Aligned with Industry Revolution 4.0 (IR 4.0) and sustainability principles, ASTECH 2024 encourages exploration of state-of-art technologies in engineering, technology, sciences, TVET, and education.

As the primary sponsor for ASTECH 2024, the Ministry of Education, Innovation & Talent Development Sarawak, (MEITD) highlights its commitment to fostering education, innovation and talent development, essential for educational excellence, future-proof workforce and sustainable growth in the region. By supporting initiatives such as WorldSkills Malaysia Sarawak, MEITD advocates for vocational, technological, and service-oriented education and talent development. WorldSkills Malaysia, an international organization, hosts biennial "Skills Olympics" competitions, where young professionals worldwide demonstrate their expertise in various skills. This initiative underscores the importance of skilled professionals in economic development and personal success, aligning with Sarawak's mission to empower future generations through skills-based education and careers. Through these efforts, MEITD aims to create a vibrant research and innovation ecosystem, enhancing Sarawak's competitiveness globally and ensuring long-term economic prosperity.

The ASTECH 2024 Conference will host 170 research paper presentations from participants across seven countries, focusing on advancing TVET education and sustainability. The event provides a platform for educators, researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers to share insights and innovations. It includes a townhall session, an exhibition, and a forum by distinguished speakers in TVET education and sustainability, contributing to Sarawak's efforts toward achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Nearly 600 students and educators from various institutions will attend. Sponsors include Biomedix Solution Sdn Bhd and Sarawak Skills, with support from several esteemed collaborators like BIMB-EAGA, Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM), Universiti Malaysia Sarawak (UNIMAS), SIDMA College Sarawak, Association of Professional Technicians and Technologists (APTT), Technological Association Malaysia (TAM) Sarawak Branch, and Iloilo Philippine University. The conference aims to address critical issues, share best practices, and empower youth with skills and knowledge for a sustainable future.

Publication Opportunity

Please be advised that all accepted papers will be published in the ASTECH eProceeding . Selected papers will be recommended for scopus publication and the cost will be paid by authors.

*Publication is subject to further evaluation by the editor and the publication cost will be fully fund by authors.

  1. All authors should attend all the plenary sessions and their paper presentation track to eligible for a conference certificate (hybrid mode).
  2. The paper which is presented at the conference will ONLY be suggested for publication.
  3. An author can only submit a maximum of 2 papers (one as the principal author & the other as co-author).
  4. The conference registration fee will not be refunded, under any circumstances.
  5. No author name change can be changed after the paper submission.
  6. Once the paper has been sent for publication to the concerned journals/proceedings, the paper cannot be withdrawn.
  7. A paper can have maximum of 5 authors.

Publication Ethics

This Conference follows the guidelines set out by the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE) in all aspects of publication ethics, in particular, protocols of research and publication misconduct.

Author(s) :

  1. Authors attest that the material has not been previously published and that they have not transferred any rights to the article to another party.
  2. Authors should ensure the originality of their work and must properly cite others work in accordance with the approved references format.
  3. Authors should not engage in plagiarism or self-plagiarism.
  4. Authors should ensure that they follow the criteria for authorship as described in the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE).
  5. The authors must not disclose any personal information that may identify their subjects, such as overt descriptions, photographs or pedigrees. If photographs are essential to the article, the authors must receive written consent and state it clearly within the article.
  6. Authors should make all data and details of their work available to the editors if there are suspicions of data falsification or fabrication.
  7. Authors of the article should clarify any possible conflicts of interest such as their job role, research expenses, consultant expenses, and intellectual property.
  8. Read more into : https://publicationethics.org/resources/guidelines

PAPERS REVIEW PROCESS

Paper Review Process

ASTECH 2024 policy encourages the use of blind peer review and, when possible, multiple reviewers. Reviews must be conducted by expert referees who have been requested to provide unbiased and constructive comments aimed at improving the work.

Proceedings editors must take all reasonable steps to ensure the quality of the materials they publish. The ASTECH 2024 referee evaluation form will be used to review the articles.

Submission articles can be sent through Confbay Website

ASTECH 2024 EXHIBITION

ASTECH 2024 will also be complemented by an exhibition and showcase. This exhibition will feature our precious sponsors and local Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) institutions. This unique opportunity fosters an immersive learning environment for attendees. Attendees will have the unique opportunity to establish valuable connections with representatives from various industries and TVET institutions. These connections can be instrumental in career exploration, internship opportunities, or future job prospects. Other than that, attendees can explore firsthand the latest advancements in technology relevant to the technical field. This exposure allows attendees to stay abreast of industry trends and identify potential areas of specialization for their future careers. Through direct observation of impactful initiatives and products showcased by exhibitors, attendees can gain a practical understanding of how these advancements contribute to the continuous improvement and relevance of TVET programs within the Industrial Revolution 4.0 (IR4.0).
