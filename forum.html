“Unlocking Asean Worldkskills : Share Your Journey and Tips"

Panelists' Journeys and Experiences:

Real-World Applications: The panellists shared their journeys, emphasising the significance of hands-on training and real-world applications in skill development.
Collaborative Efforts: They highlighted the importance of cross-border collaboration among ASEAN countries to share resources, expertise, and best practices in vocational education.
Policy Implementation: The experiences shared included successful policy implementations and strategic planning that have contributed to the improvement of skill training and employability.

Strategies for Enhancing Workforce Efficiency:

Modernizing Training Programs: Integrating modern technology and innovative methods, such as simulation and virtual reality, into training programs to keep pace with industry advancements.
Public-Private Partnerships: Strengthening partnerships between public institutions and private sector companies to ensure that training programs are aligned with market needs.
Community-Focused Training: Tailoring skill development programs to meet the specific needs of local communities and regional industries, thereby increasing their impact and relevance.
