TOWN HALL SPEAKER

ASTECH Townhall 2024

  • Date: 8th August 2024
  • Venue: Hilton Hotel Kuching, Sarawak, Malaysia

ASTECH Townhall, a sub-event of ASTECH 2024, serves as a pivotal platform for engaging and involving various stakeholders, including industries, government sectors, and academia, in discussions surrounding a sustainable and environmentally conscious future. In alignment with the overarching theme of ASTECH 2024, distinguished speakers hailing from diverse backgrounds will center their discussions on the topic of "Pioneering Solutions for Achieving Carbon Neutrality: Shaping a Tech-Infused Tomorrow." The anticipated outcome of this townhall event is to facilitate collaboration, the exchange of vital information, and collective endeavors aimed at fostering a positive impact on both the community and the environment. Anticipated to draw a diverse, multinational audience, this townhall promises to provide valuable insights into the multifaceted contributions of various stakeholders in achieving the ambitious Net-Zero emission targets.
