TOWN HALL SPEAKER

ASTECH Townhall 2024

  • Date: 8th August 2024
  • Venue: Hilton Hotel Kuching, Sarawak, Malaysia

“Future Talent Synergy: Unveiling Excellence in TVET, WorldSkills, and Industries"

ASTECH Townhall, a pivotal sub-event of ASTECH 2024, strategically aligns with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), emphasizing Goal 17: Partnerships for the Goals. This academic forum unites stakeholders from diverse sectors—industries, government, and academia—with a focus on forging global partnerships for sustainable development. Themed "Future Talent Synergy: Shaping Tomorrow's Workforce," the town hall addresses SDG 4 (Quality Education). Esteemed contributors, including our TVET Professor and WorldSkills Competition Coach, articulate sophisticated perspectives on the role of technical and vocational education in cultivating an inclusive workforce. Simultaneously, the Skills Department and Industry Representatives offer insights into the intricate interplay of education, skills, and industry voices. This enriching exchange fosters collaborative synergies and contributes substantively to SDGs 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth) and 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure), marking significant strides towards global sustainability objectives.
